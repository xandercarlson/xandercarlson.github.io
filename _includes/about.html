About

Originally residing in Atlanta, GA, Xander Carlson has always been fond of building and designing creating a wide variety of things in the workshop. After graduating highschool and moving up to Wake Forest, NC in 2019, he began working on his Associate's Degree and continued to work in various positions that allowed him to continue his quest for growth and knowledge. After being accepted into the Reich School of Education, at Appalachian State University, he achieved his Bachelor's Degree in Technology, Engineering, and Design Education.
